# How to Safely Dispose of Lithium Batteries

Damaged or old batteries can cause fire hazards, so they must be disposed of in a special way. They also contain heavy metals such as cadmium, which are harmful to the environment.

## Discharging

First, you need to discharge the cells. This can be done by connecting them to a halogen or incandescent lamp for an extended period. Some chargers also have a dedicated full-discharge “storage/disposal” mode.
